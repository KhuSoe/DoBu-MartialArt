This is DOBU

"DoBu is a modern martial art that melds aspects from different traditional martial arts to form a flexible and inclusive self-defense method. Combining striking and grappling techniques, DoBu includes punches, kicks, joint locks, throws, and ground fighting. Its emphasis is on practical self-defense, adaptability, and smooth movements, making it effective in real-life scenarios. Practitioners of DoBu strive to enhance physical fitness, mental strength, and tactical understanding, merging martial arts philosophy with contemporary training techniques to create a comprehensive martial arts practitioner."

Popular Classes

Judo, established by Jigoro Kano in 1882, is a Japanese martial art and Olympic sport that centers on throwing and grappling techniques, with a focus on leveraging an opponent's momentum and strength.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), derived from Japanese Jujutsu and made famous by the Gracie family, is a martial art specializing in ground fighting and submission techniques, enabling smaller practitioners to defend themselves effectively.

Karate, originating in the Ryukyu Kingdom, is a traditional Japanese martial art that emphasizes striking techniques and open-hand strikes, highlighting the importance of physical fitness and mental discipline.
